Game Rules

Welcome to Popix Mobile!

Your objective is to pop all the circles as quickly as possible.

  • Tap on the circles in order from 20 down to 1.
  • Each missed tap adds a 0.5-second penalty to your final time.
  • Try to achieve the fastest time possible!

Good luck and have fun!
