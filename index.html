Game Rules

  1. Objective: Pop as many circles as possible within the allotted time by tapping or clicking on them.
  2. How to Play:
    • Start the Game: Tap the Start button to begin.
    • Pop Circles: A single circle will appear on the screen. Tap or click on it to pop.
    • Avoid Misses: Clicking or tapping outside the circle counts as a miss and will add a slight penalty to your total time.
    • Progression: After popping a circle, a new one will appear in a different location.
    • End of Game: The game ends after you pop a predefined number of circles (e.g., 10) or when time runs out.
  3. Scoring:
    • Fast Pops: Pop circles quickly to achieve a better score.
    • Penalties: Each miss adds a small penalty to your total time, affecting your overall score.
  4. Leaderboard:
    • Compete Globally: Compare your scores with players worldwide.
    • Submit Your Score: After the game, enter your name to save your score to the leaderboard.
  5. Additional Features:
    • Sound Effects: Enjoy satisfying pop sounds when you successfully pop a circle.
    • Vibration Feedback: Feel a subtle vibration on mobile devices upon popping a circle.
  6. Tips for Success:
    • Stay Focused: Concentrate on the active circle to minimize misses.
    • Quick Reflexes: The faster you pop circles, the higher your score will be.
    • Manage Misses: Avoid unnecessary taps or clicks to keep your total time low.
